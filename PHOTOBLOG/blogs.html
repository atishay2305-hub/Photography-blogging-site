How to get started?

In the year 2020, you don't need a high end DSLR or a mirrorless camera to take mind blowing images. The only tool you actually need is your smartphone. Smartphone photography has taken a rise in the industry in the recent years quiet a bit. Nowadays every smartphone, even the lower end models have a feature called "PRO" mode built into the camera app which takes photography to the next level for the smartphone photographers. It lets you take advantage of the pro features like ISO, Aperture, White Balance. Now it won't be of that quality of what you get from a DSLR or mirrorless but it is great enough to get started.
